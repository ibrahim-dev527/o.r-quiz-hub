Prof. Ernest C. Winful

Professor of Operations Research and Quantitative Methods

Prof. Ernest C. Winful is a distinguished academic with over 20 years of experience teaching Operations Research at the university level. His expertise spans optimization techniques, stochastic modeling, and decision analysis with applications in supply chain management, healthcare systems, and financial engineering. He is renowned for his ability to make complex quantitative concepts accessible and engaging for students at all levels.

Throughout his career, Prof. Winful has been committed to developing innovative teaching methodologies that emphasize practical problem-solving skills alongside theoretical foundations. His quiz-based learning approach has been instrumental in helping thousands of students master challenging Operations Research concepts and achieve academic excellence.
